Centsible

Centsible

According to Gallup only 54% of Americans currently invest. If this trend persists, income inequality in this country will to continue to increase. Centisble is a financial investment learning app that uses gamification to gradually introduce users to basic investing terminology and strategy. Users get $5000 to invest in a fictional financial market based on historical data and see how much they can grow their portfolio over the course of the 10-year lifecycle of the app. Investing can be intimidating, so Centsible tries to help users make a little more sense out of it.


React, Redux, Node JS, Passport, Mocha, Chai, MongoDB

Fantasy Congress 2018

Fantasy Congress 2018

Fantasy Congress 2018 is a political engagement app modeled on Daily Fantasy Sports where users create teams comprised of congressional candidates who are priced based on their expected success in the election. The teams are then scored based on how well the candidates on their team do on Election Night. This app gives users a good reason to dig into the data and put together the best team of candidates to determine who really knows the most about politics.


React, Redux, Node JS, Passport, MongoDB

Splorin'

Splorin'

Splorin' is a travel inspiration mobile app where users enter their travel dates and then swipe through images from some of the most breathtaking vacation destinations around the world. By plugging into the Kiwi API, if a user is feeling inspired, all it takes is a swipe to the right and they will see flight costs to the desired destination while a left swipe will bring up a new travel destination. Have fun Splorin' for your next vacation destination!


React Native, Redux

Wishlist

Wishlist

Wishlist is an app that allows users to record restaurants they want to visit. Plugging into the Google Maps and Google Places APIs, the app automatically stores a restaurant's crucial information like location and hours, while also allowing the user to add their own notes based on friend's recommendations or their own personal experiences. Never again forget the name of that great spot your friend said you have to try!

SATutor

SATutor

SATutor is an app built to help students improve their vocabulary for the Verbal portion of the SATs. It is built on a spaced-repeitition algorithm to optimize a student's capacity to memorize new words without forgetting the words they have previously learned. SATutor will do all the work in terms of determining when a student should revisit a word they previously learned in order to not forget it come test time.


React, Redux, Node JS, Passport, MongoDB
